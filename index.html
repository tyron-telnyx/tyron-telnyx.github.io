Grow , Glow!

In a damned and ghoulish desire for short sighted profit and exorbitant luxury, impecunious souls not only make life unbearable in the present, they steal from future generations. Will they succeed in leaving all land bare and barren?

This is the time for great bravery and virtue for all who choose to be foresters. Only the passionately strategic and objective know that the good of Igbo! is good for all of us, because when the land is green, all its occupants are glad!

While it is all our duty to care for the earth, we need a brave and strategic leader to inspire us and show us how to make the most efficient use of the natural resources that we share.

Do you have what it takes?

Rise to the challenge and prove your planting prowess!

Place

Either plant a new seed on the board or choose to move a planted seed one space in any direction with the goal of forming triples in either lines of 3 (1 point), 6 (3 points), or 9 (5 points) or boxes of 6 (2*3 points) or 9 (3*3: 5 points).

Flip

A series of 3 consecutive planted seeds means that it is time for your seeds to germinate and grow into lush, green trees. Flip over each seed in the series to reveal the mighty grove you have grown!

Rearrange

The round ends when one player has exhausted all of their seeds. You now get the chance to reorder their combinations where careful thought is needed to reveal new opportunites for more points that were previously unseen during the round

Count

Take turns removing scoring combinations from the board (Beware! Removing one combination might break another!), totalling up the scores of each player as you go. Shrewd allocation of resources is rewarded by adding 1 point to your score for each chip that you did not place on the board
